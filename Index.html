Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Snake Reborn ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Snake Reborn is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your high score
  • Game speed preference
  • Music preference (on/off)
  • Whether the tutorial has been shown
  • In-app purchase (tip) status

This data is stored using your device's local storage (UserDefaults) and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional tip-based in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, billing details, or Apple ID. All purchase transactions are governed by Apple's own privacy policy and terms of service.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, advertising frameworks, or third-party SDKs that collect user data. The App does not track your activity in any way.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data. The only external interaction is with Apple's StoreKit for processing optional in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can remove it at any time by deleting the App. No data is retained by us after the App is removed from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
